Mission
=======

OUR MISSION

We believe that Poverty is rooted in inequality, and in human action or inaction and therefore our mission is to ensure that our Kenyan communities have a just environment without poverty.

This can be achieved by ensuring that they are valued and treated equally through enjoying their rights as full citizens, and can also influence decisions affecting their lives.

Our main objective is to help create lasting solutions to the injustice of poverty.

We also hope to unleash people's potential to create a positive change through the use of a combination of rights-based sustainable development programs, public education, campaigns, advocacy, and humanitarian assistance in disasters and conflicts.

We challenge the structural causes of the injustice of poverty,

and work with allies and partners locally and globally.
